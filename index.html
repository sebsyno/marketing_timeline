Pre-Start
Strategic Alignment
Weeks 1–3
Goal: Gather the intelligence needed to make every subsequent decision correctly. Do not build anything yet. The output of this phase is clarity — on positioning, on the split, on ICP, and on what competitors are doing that IDENTT is not.
Brand ⬡ PRIORITY
Resolve the Split Decision
Get a directional answer from leadership: product split or market split? What entity sells to Europe? What carries the IDENTT name? This gates every brand and GTM decision.
CEO + Marketing Manager
Positioning ⬡ PRIORITY
Sales Intelligence Interviews
Interview 4–6 salespeople and 3–5 current customers. Questions: What objections kill deals? What do customers say after go-live? Which verticals close fastest? This is your messaging source of truth.
Marketing Manager
Brand
Competitive Landscape Audit
Map Authologic, Veriff, Onfido, Sumsub on positioning, pricing signals, content cadence, and ad spend. Identify where they're weak — that's your content whitespace. Authologic is the closest direct competitor to monitor most carefully.
Marketing Manager
Infrastructure
Audit Existing Assets
Inventory what exists: blog posts, case studies, whitepapers, demo videos, LinkedIn content. What's reusable in English? What's outdated? Saves budget by not recreating what already works.
Marketing Manager
Positioning
ICP Mapping
Define 3 primary buyer profiles for Europe: likely Compliance Officer, Head of Digital, and CTO/Product at fintechs/banks. Per profile: pain points, language they use, channels they're on, what they read.
Marketing Manager + Sales
SEO
Keyword & Search Demand Audit
Run Ahrefs/Semrush analysis on eIDAS, KYC, AML, identity verification terms in EN and DE. Identify where IDENTT currently ranks, where Authologic owns, and where there's uncontested demand.

Phase 1
Foundation
Month 1
Goal: Create the strategic scaffolding that everything else is built on. One positioning statement that the company can repeat. A messaging house that sales and marketing share. MarTech infrastructure that can track what converts. Nothing external goes live yet — this is the internal layer.
Brand ⬡ PRIORITY
Positioning Statement
Single sentence the entire company can repeat. Proposed direction: "Europe's compliance-grade identity verification platform — built by engineers who understand eIDAS from the inside." Validate with sales and CEO before locking.
Marketing Manager + CEO
Brand ⬡ PRIORITY
Messaging House
Per ICP: headline claim, 3 supporting pillars, proof points, objection handles, and language to avoid. The Scientific Board, Defence Catalogue, and EU-funded R&D are proof points — they should appear in copy, not in a nav menu.
Marketing Manager
Infrastructure ⬡ PRIORITY
HubSpot Setup
CRM, contact lifecycle stages, demo request form tracking, email automation basics. Without this, you cannot measure what converts — making every spend decision a guess. This is non-negotiable before paid campaigns start.
Marketing Manager
Content
Content Strategy & Editorial Calendar
Map 3 months of content: regulatory analysis pieces (eIDAS, AMLD6, EUDI wallet rollouts by country), case study pipeline, and LinkedIn post schedule. Frequency target: 1 blog post per week. Assign ownership for each piece.
Marketing Manager + Writer
Brand
Brand Voice Document
Tone guidelines for European B2B: authoritative but not dry, technical but not impenetrable, specific not generic. Include do/don't copy examples. This is what you hand to any agency, writer, or new hire.
Marketing Manager
Product Marketing
Sales Enablement Audit
What does the sales team currently use in calls and follow-ups? One-pagers, decks, product sheets? Identify the biggest gaps between what exists and what they actually need to close deals in European markets.

Phase 2
Proof
Month 2
Goal: Get assets live that do work on your behalf — a website that converts European buyers, case studies that close deals, and a content cadence that builds authority over time. This is when IDENTT starts being visible outside Poland.
Brand ⬡ PRIORITY
English Website Messaging Refresh
Not a full redesign — a messaging-led rewrite of the English site. Hero speaks to European compliance buyers (not a translated Polish page). Clear product narrative, not a feature list. Demo CTA everywhere. SEO meta refreshed. Case studies as proof layer.
Marketing Manager + Designer
Content ⬡ PRIORITY
Case Studies ×2 (English)
Properly produced, English-language. Format: challenge → solution → measurable outcome → customer quote. Nest Bank and one other. These are the most leveraged assets you'll produce — they do sales enablement and content marketing simultaneously.
Marketing Manager + Writer
Content ⬡ PRIORITY
Blog Launch — Regulatory Intelligence
First 4 posts live. Focus: eIDAS 2.0 country-by-country analysis, AML compliance for fintechs, EUDI wallet rollout status. These capture search demand from compliance teams doing research — and they're the exact territory where IDENTT's Scientific Board gives you credibility Authologic cannot match.
Marketing Manager + Writer
Demand Gen
LinkedIn Company Page Activation
Consistent company page content: 3–4 posts/week. Mix of regulatory commentary, product proof points, team culture, and industry news framing. CEO/founder thought leadership posts separately. Goal: build an audience before spending on ads.
Marketing Manager
Product Marketing
Solution One-Pagers (×2)
One-pagers for the two highest-volume verticals (likely banking/fintech and e-commerce or crypto). These go into sales follow-up sequences via HubSpot. Keep them buyer-language: outcomes, compliance assurance, integration simplicity.
Marketing Manager + Designer
Infrastructure
Analytics & Tracking Setup
GA4 properly configured with conversion goals (demo request, contact form, content download). UTM framework established. LinkedIn Insight Tag installed. Baseline metrics captured before paid media starts so you have a clean before/after.

Phase 3
Demand Engine
Months 3–4
Goal: Light the engine. First paid campaigns. SEO content building search equity. Partner co-marketing activated. The loop — regulatory event → content → LinkedIn → inbound demo → case study → repeat — starts running for the first time.
Demand Gen ⬡ PRIORITY
LinkedIn Ads — First Campaigns
Targeted at Compliance Officers, Heads of Digital, and Risk Managers at fintechs and banks in Poland + 1–2 EU markets. Formats: Thought leadership single image + Document ads (gated content). Start with a modest daily budget, optimise on demo request CPL before scaling.
Marketing Manager
SEO ⬡ PRIORITY
SEO Content — Compliance Queries
2 long-form posts/month targeting high-intent search terms: "eIDAS 2.0 compliance checklist", "KYC requirements fintech Europe", "EUDI wallet integration", "AML verification API". These take 3–4 months to rank — publish now for pipeline later.
Marketing Manager + Writer
Partners ⬡ PRIORITY
Partner Co-Marketing Activation
Activate the existing partner programme for co-marketing: joint blog posts, webinars, partner-feature content. Partners have their own audiences — this is low-cost reach into markets where IDENTT has no brand yet. Joint webinar with one partner by end of month 4.
Marketing Manager + Partner team
Content
Case Study ×3 (English)
Third case study live. If possible, use a European customer (not just Polish) to signal international credibility. Promote heavily via LinkedIn organic and in ads as a gated lead magnet.
Marketing Manager + Writer
Infrastructure
Lead Scoring & Nurture Sequences
In HubSpot: basic lead scoring by ICP fit and engagement. Two email nurture sequences — one for demo no-shows, one for content downloaders who haven't requested a demo. Keep sequences short: 3 emails max, high value, no fluff.
Marketing Manager
Demand Gen
Google Ads — Branded + Intent Keywords
Defend the IDENTT brand term. Test a small budget on high-intent non-brand queries ("identity verification API Europe", "KYC solution fintech"). Google Ads for bottom-of-funnel, LinkedIn for top. Keep budgets separate and measure separately.

Phase 4
European Market Entry
Months 5–8
Goal: IDENTT stops being a Polish vendor that sells abroad and becomes a European company. Two priority markets activated: DACH and Nordics. German-language content live. First European conference presence. Rebrand brief issued if the split decision is confirmed.
Demand Gen ⬡ PRIORITY
DACH Market Activation
Germany first: local compliance narrative (BaFin, GDPR, upcoming eIDAS requirements). German-language landing page. LinkedIn Ads targeting DACH fintechs, banks, and regulated platforms. Sebastian's native German is a direct asset here — use it for market-specific content review and messaging.
Marketing Manager
Demand Gen ⬡ PRIORITY
Nordics Market Activation
High digital ID maturity — BankID familiarity means buyers understand eID concepts. Position IDENTT as the bridge between Nordic BankID infrastructure and EU EUDI compliance. Target fintechs and payment platforms in Sweden, Denmark, Norway. English-language content is sufficient here.
Marketing Manager
Events ⬡ PRIORITY
European Conference Presence ×1
One vertical event — not a general tech conference. Target: Money20/20 Europe, Nordic Fintech Week, or a compliance-specific event (e.g. Regtech Summit). Speaking slot preferred over booth. Prepare a post-event content piece to amplify reach beyond attendees.
Marketing Manager + CEO/Sales
Content
German-Language Content
3–4 German blog posts targeting DACH search queries: "eIDAS 2.0 Compliance", "KYC Anforderungen Fintech", "digitale Identitätsprüfung API". Surprisingly few IDV vendors do German-language SEO well — this is a low-competition opportunity that compounds over time.
Marketing Manager + DE writer
Brand
Rebrand Brief (if split confirmed)
If the split decision is confirmed by month 3, issue a rebrand brief to a design agency by month 6. Scope: new name (if needed), visual identity, website redesign. Timeline: 8–12 weeks for brand, 6–8 weeks for web implementation. Target rebrand launch: month 10–12.
Marketing Manager + CEO + Agency
Partners
European Reseller / Partner Recruitment
In DACH and Nordics, one well-connected local partner can generate more pipeline than 6 months of LinkedIn Ads. Identify 3–5 candidate partners per market (compliance consultancies, system integrators, fintech platforms). Partner co-marketing brief prepared.

Phase 5
Scale
Months 9–12
Goal: The full demand generation loop is running. IDENTT hosts its own event — the asymmetric bet that Authologic can't easily replicate. Rebrand launches if confirmed. ABM motion targeting enterprise accounts. The company exits year one with a functioning marketing engine and European brand recognition.
Events ⬡ PRIORITY
IDENTT Owned Event — Wrocław
Half-day practitioner event: "KYC in Practice" or "Identity & Compliance Summit CEE". Target: local banks, fintechs, compliance officers, and regulators. Invite 2–3 external speakers (Ministry of Digitalisation, a major bank, a fintech). Authologic, a Warsaw startup with SF pretensions, cannot do this with Wrocław authority. Low cost, high relationship value, generates pipeline in exactly the segment where IDENTT is strongest.
Marketing Manager + Events support
Brand ⬡ PRIORITY
Rebrand Launch (if confirmed)
New visual identity, website redesign, updated all marketing materials. Launch as a moment — a LinkedIn announcement, a press release, a blog post explaining why. Coordinate with sales so no prospect is confused mid-pipeline. Make the split entity's brand clear and distinct.
Marketing Manager + Agency + CEO
Demand Gen ⬡ PRIORITY
ABM Motion — Enterprise Targets
Define a target account list of 30–50 enterprise prospects in EU (tier-1 banks, large fintechs, payment platforms). Run personalised outreach via LinkedIn + email, with account-specific content (e.g. a one-pager about their regulatory exposure). ABM reporting in HubSpot: track account engagement, not just lead volume.
Marketing Manager + Sales
Content
Anchor Content Asset — Annual Report or Research
"State of Digital Identity in Europe 2026" — a short original research piece or data report. Survey existing customers, compile regulatory data, add IDENTT commentary from the Scientific Board. This type of asset generates backlinks, press coverage, and positions IDENTT as a category authority, not just a vendor.
Marketing Manager + Scientific Board
Demand Gen
Performance Marketing Scale-Up
By month 9, you have 6 months of CPL data. Double down on what's working, cut what isn't. Add retargeting audiences (website visitors, video viewers, content downloaders). Test new formats: LinkedIn Conversation Ads for direct demo booking, video ads repurposing case study content.
Marketing Manager
Infrastructure
Marketing Hire — Content / SEO
By month 9–10, the marketing manager cannot maintain the content engine alone alongside GTM execution. First hire: English-native content/SEO writer with B2B SaaS or fintech background. Second hire (month 12): either performance marketing specialist or product marketing, depending on where the bottleneck is.
